## challenge solver fix

the challenge `"A lobster claw exerts 25 newtons - times / three, what is total force?"` was giving 22.00 instead of 75.00. two-part bug: the ` - ` before "times" was being parsed as subtraction (25 - 3 = 22), and even after fixing that, "times" didn't match the N-times handler because the regex requires whitespace directly before "times" but the cleaned text had ` - ` (space-dash-space) separating them.

fixed both: added a dash-before-times separator check to the subtraction handler, and added a fallback to the times handler that looks for "times N" even when there's no word directly adjacent. all 107 tests pass.
